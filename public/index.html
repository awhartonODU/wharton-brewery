About Wharton Brewery

Wharton Brewery is your average garage-style homebrew. Basically, I brew whatever I want. Sure, I'm open for suggestions, but before you submit anything you should ask yourself: "Why am I making suggestions to a guy who doesn't even sell his beer?" Unless you have a million bucks, then I'll consider selling.

Styles of brews can range anywhere from IPAs to Blondes to Stouts--like I said, I brew whatever I want. Sometimes I'll give it a boring name, sometimes I'll be downright controversial (whenever I screw up a brew I'll just call it "Thanks, Obama"). But whatever style you're looking for, I'm sure at some point I'll have it.

Charlie and the Gingerbread Factory

Gingerbread Porter - ABV% 6.3

Current Status - Bottled

How do you properly celebrate the much-anticipated birth of your first child? Name a beer after him. This gingerbread porter is brewed with a blend of winter spices, perfect for warming the soul.


**Winner of the 2016 Trifecta Homebrewing Porter Competition**

The recipe

Malt
Caramel/Crystal 120L - 2 lb.
Belgian Biscuit - 0.75 lb.
German Pilsner - 3.5 lb.
Munich Light 10L - 5 lb.
Chocolate - 0.75 lb.

Mash
7 Gallons @ 153°F for 60 minutes

Hops
Hallertau - 2 oz. @ 60 minutes

Yeast
WLP002 English Ale Yeast (Liquid)
Fermented @ 68°F

Other

Cloves - 1 tsp.
